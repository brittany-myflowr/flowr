About flowr

flowr is a self-care routine planner for iOS that helps you actually implement the routines you've been collecting. Built for Millennial and Gen Z women who treat self-care as a ritual, flowr gives your routines a home — out of your screenshots and notes and into your real life.

What flowr does

  • Build routines across 10 categories — Skincare, Body Care, Hair Care, Nail Care, Oral Care, Supplements, Medications, Exercise, Wellness, and Other
  • Schedule each routine your way — daily, weekly, monthly, or whenever you want. It's completely customizable
  • Customize each step individually — because not every step belongs on the same schedule
  • Tag products to routine steps and rate them (Love It, Like It, Not For Me)
  • Sync routines to your cycle — supports both menstrual and lunar cycle tracking
  • See exactly what's coming up today — morning, afternoon, and evening
  • Toggle any routine on or off without losing it — switch seasonally, test something new without deleting the old, or pause when life gets busy
  • Duplicate any routine to use as a starting point for something similar, so you never have to build from scratch
  • Browse your full self-care calendar — plan ahead and look back
  • 14-day free trial, no credit card required. And when your trial ends, nothing happens automatically

About the Founder

Brittany Theodore is the founder of flowr and the sole developer behind Petal Studios LLC, a Delaware-based independent software company. She built flowr because she kept saving self-care routines she wanted to start — only to lose track or forget all about them. flowr is her answer to that problem, and she hopes it becomes yours too.
